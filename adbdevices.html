Procedure: Pulling boot.img from Realme Note 50 (Android 15)

Important Warnings

  • Unlocking or modifying partitions may void your warranty or trigger SafetyNet.
  • Extracting or flashing the wrong boot.img can brick your phoneâ€”proceed with caution.
  • Back up your data first.

Requirements

  1. Computer with ADB & Fastboot installed: Download here.
  2. Enable Developer Options and USB Debugging on your phone.
  3. Use the original or a high-quality USB cable.

Steps to Pull boot.img

1. adb devices
2. adb reboot bootloader
3. fastboot devices
4. fastboot getvar all
5. fastboot fetch boot boot.imgIf fetch not supported:

adb shell su dd if=/dev/block/bootdevice/by-name/boot of=/sdcard/boot.img exit adb pull /sdcard/boot.img

6. file boot.img

Alternative

If blocked, download official Realme firmware for your exact build and extract boot.img using payload-dumper-go or MTKclient.
